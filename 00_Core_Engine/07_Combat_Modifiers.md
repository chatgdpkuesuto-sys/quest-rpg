# Combat Modifiers

## Cover
Walls, trees, creatures, and other obstacles can provide cover, making a target harder to harm.

### Half Cover
- **Benefit**: +2 Bonus to AC and DEX Saving Throws.
- **Criteria**: Target is at least half blocked (e.g., low wall, creature in between).

### Three-Quarters Cover
- **Benefit**: +5 Bonus to AC and DEX Saving Throws.
- **Criteria**: Target is about 3/4 blocked (e.g., arrow slit, thick tree trunk).

### Total Cover
- **Benefit**: Cannot be targeted directly by attacks or spells.
- **Criteria**: Completely concealed by an obstacle.

## Vision and Light
- **Lightly Obscured** (Dim light, patchy fog): **Disadvantage** on Perception checks relying on sight.
- **Heavily Obscured** (Darkness, thick fog): Effectively **Blinded** (see `08_Conditions.md`).

## Damage Types
- **Physical**: Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing.
- **Elemental**: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Thunder.
- **Exotic**: Force, Necrotic, Psychic, Radiant, Poison.

### Resistance & Vulnerability
- **Resistance**: Takes **half damage**.
- **Vulnerability**: Takes **double damage**.
- **Immunity**: Takes **no damage**.
